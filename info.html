About Us

Misfits martial arts is a family friendly and excitment-filled martial arts club. From ages of 4 up to 104, it's never too early, or too late to learn to defend yourself, or to get healthy!

Whether you are a fitness guru, or an ice cream eating couch potato, there is something for everyone! It is down to your own ability and how hard you push yourself. The only competition is the competition that you give yourself.

With over 50 years combined experience of teaching and training, there is always something to learn at every class.
